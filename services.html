Frequently Asked Questions

What services are available at Shankar Diabetes Care Centre?

At Shankar Diabetes Care Centre, we provide comprehensive care for diabetes, thyroid disorders, hormonal imbalance, hypertension, obesity management, diabetic complications, preventive health check-ups, lifestyle counseling, and long-term diabetes management.

Who should visit a diabetes specialist?

People experiencing high blood sugar, frequent urination, excessive thirst, sudden weight changes, fatigue, thyroid-related symptoms, obesity, or hormonal imbalance should consult a diabetes and endocrinology specialist for proper evaluation and treatment.

Do you provide treatment for both Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes?

Yes, we offer personalized treatment and management plans for both Type 1 and Type 2 diabetes, including medication guidance, insulin management, diet counseling, and lifestyle modification support.

Can diabetes be managed without complications?

Yes, with regular monitoring, proper medication, healthy lifestyle habits, and timely medical consultation, diabetes can be effectively managed and the risk of complications can be significantly reduced.

What diabetic complications do you treat?

We provide monitoring and management for diabetic complications such as diabetic neuropathy (nerve damage), nephropathy (kidney-related complications), and retinopathy (eye-related complications).

Do you provide thyroid and hormonal disorder treatment?

Yes, we diagnose and treat thyroid disorders, hormonal imbalance, metabolic conditions, and endocrine-related health issues with personalized care plans.

Why is preventive health check-up important for diabetes patients?

Preventive health check-ups help in early detection of diabetes-related complications, blood pressure issues, obesity risks, and hormonal disorders, allowing timely treatment and better long-term health outcomes.

Can obesity and hypertension increase the risk of diabetes?

Yes, obesity and high blood pressure are major risk factors for diabetes and other metabolic disorders. Proper lifestyle management and medical supervision can help control these conditions effectively.

Do you provide diet and lifestyle counseling?

Yes, our diabetes care approach includes diet guidance, exercise recommendations, weight management support, and lifestyle counseling to help patients maintain healthy blood sugar levels.

Why choose Shankar Diabetes Care Centre?

Shankar Diabetes Care Centre has been dedicated to comprehensive diabetes and endocrine care since 2002, offering experienced medical guidance, personalized treatment plans, preventive care, and patient-focused management for long-term health and wellness.
