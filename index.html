About Us

About Shankar Diabetes Care Centre.

Since 2002, Shankar Diabetes Care Centre has been committed to delivering comprehensive and personalized diabetes care. Founded by Prof. Dr. Anand Shankar (M.D. Medicine), our centre focuses on improving your quality of life through accurate diagnosis, treatment, and continuous care.
Mission : To provide specialized and affordable diabetes care for a healthier, happier future.

  • Advanced diagnostics including FibroScan, Lunar DXA, ECG, and Fundus examination
  • Accurate diagnosis supported by modern medical technology
  • Evidence-based treatment for effective diabetes management
  • Focus on controlling blood sugar levels consistently
  • Emphasis on overall health improvement
  • Prevention of long-term diabetes complications
  • Continuous care with regular monitoring and follow-ups
  • Guidance on lifestyle and daily habits for better living

Frequently Asked Questions

What services are offered at Shankar Diabetes Care Centre?

We provide comprehensive diabetes care including diabetes consultation, blood sugar management, thyroid and hormonal disorder treatment, hypertension and obesity management, diabetic complication screening, preventive health check-ups, and advanced diagnostic facilities.

Who is Dr. Anand Shankar?

Dr. Anand Shankar is an experienced Diabetes & Endocrinology specialist with over 24 years of expertise in managing diabetes, thyroid disorders, and hormonal health. He has also served as a consultant at Apollo Hospital, Chennai and Sir Ganga Ram Hospital, Delhi.

What types of diabetes do you treat?

We provide personalized treatment and management plans for Type 1 Diabetes, Type 2 Diabetes, Gestational Diabetes, and other endocrine-related metabolic disorders.

What advanced diagnostic facilities are available at the centre?

Our centre offers advanced facilities including FibroScan, Lunar DXA Scanner, ECG, Diabetic Foot Check-up, Fundus Examination, and other diagnostic services for comprehensive diabetes care.

Why is regular diabetes screening important?

Regular diabetes screening helps in early detection of complications related to the eyes, kidneys, nerves, heart, and liver, allowing timely treatment and better long-term health outcomes.

Do you provide treatment for thyroid and hormonal disorders?

Yes, we specialize in diagnosing and treating thyroid disorders, hormonal imbalances, obesity-related endocrine conditions, and other endocrinology concerns.

How can I book an appointment at Shankar Diabetes Care Centre?

You can book an appointment by calling us at +91-7857811112 or +91-7070992526, or by using the “Book Appointment” option available on our website.

Where is Shankar Diabetes Care Centre located?

Shankar Diabetes Care Centre is conveniently located in Rajendra Nagar, Patna, making it easily accessible for patients across Bihar.

What lifestyle changes help in diabetes management?

A healthy diet, regular exercise, timely medication, stress management, weight control, and routine monitoring of blood sugar levels are essential for effective diabetes management.

Why choose Shankar Diabetes Care Centre for diabetes care?

Since 2002, Shankar Diabetes Care Centre has been dedicated to providing trusted, personalized, and affordable diabetes care with experienced medical guidance, advanced diagnostic technology, and patient-focused treatment plans.
